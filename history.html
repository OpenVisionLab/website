Project History

A timeline of our VisionLab development journey.

Week 1 – Idea & Planning

We discussed different project ideas and decided to create VisionLab. Our goal was to build a simple and clean website to present our work.

Week 2 – Website Design

We designed the structure of the main page, including the header, navigation, and layout. We focused on making the website visually clear and easy to use.

Week 3 – Development

We started building the website using HTML and CSS. We created the main page and added different sections like About, Project, and Team.

Week 4 – Individual Pages

We created individual pages for each team member and connected them to the main page. This helped organize team information clearly.

Week 5 – Final Improvements

We improved the design, added project pages such as Process and Result, and fixed small issues. Finally, we uploaded our project to GitHub.
